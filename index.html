IEEE VTC Workshop on Generative AI and Large Model Empowered Next Generation Networks

19 October 2025 (Sunday)
Chengdu, China

Call for Workshop Papers

In the dynamic landscape of communication networks, traditional Artificial Intelligence (AI) algorithms have been instrumental, optimizing and troubleshooting functions within communication networks. However, as 6G networks demand higher performance, traditional AI models face limitations. Breakthroughs in Generative AI (GAI) and large model, including transformer-based models like GPT-3.5 and GPT-4, offer the ability to support customization and data generation, introducing a new dimension to communication networks with dynamic adaptation. GAI and large model enhance content creation, network planning, and infrastructure deployment, improving efficiency and user experience. As GAI and large model integrate into next-generation networks, they drive innovation, adaptability, and personalized experiences in communication. At the same time, integrating GAI and large models into next-generation networks requires careful consideration of how to optimize the corresponding communication technologies to ensure their reliable operation and high performance. This proposed half-day workshop will seek to bring together researchers and experts from academia, industry, and governmental agencies to discuss and promote the research and development needed to overcome the major challenges that pertain to this cutting-edge research topic. Suitable topics for this workshop include, but are not limited to, the following areas:

Suitable topics include:

  • Generative AI (GAI)/large models for vehicular networks
  • GAI/large models for space-air-ground networks
  • GAI/large models for semantic communication and pragmatic communication systems
  • GAI/large model-driven resource allocation and spectrum management
  • Traffic prediction and congestion control using GAI/large models
  • Automated network configuration and optimization with GAI/large models
  • Network security and privacy enhancements through GAI/large models
  • Architectures and protocols for in-network GAI/large model training and inference
  • Deploying GAI/large model in cloud-edge networks
  • Experimental prototyping and testbeds for GAI/large model-assisted networks
